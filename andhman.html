  • Day1-  Port blair arrival , your 7 days and 6 nights starts from there On arrival in Andaman, the agent's representative will greet you at the Port Blair and arrange for a smooth transfer to your hotel. The first day is available for leisure. You can head out for some sightseeing or just relax in the hotel with your partner.In the evening, get ready for a visit to the Cellular Jail. Take a guide along to know about the history of this world-famous prison. After the sunset, the jail comes alive with patriotic songs and a poignant narration of the freedom movement presented in the form of a Light and Sound Show.After the show, board the cab to the hotel for a cozy overnight stay at the hotel.



  • Day2-  Have your breakfast in the hotel and then get ready for a day tour of Port Blair. On the second day of your Andaman honeymoon tour, you will get to visit attractions like the famous Wandoor Beach and the Chidiya Tapu.It starts with the beautiful and secluded beach of Wandoor. You can also visit the nearby Marine Park which houses a large diversity of marine species. Towards afternoon, start for Chidiya Tapu which is south of Port Blair. Chidiya Tapu is one of the best places to watch the sunset in Port Blair. After enjoying the sunset with your beloved, return to the hotel for a comfy overnight stay.


  • Day3-  After breakfast at the hotel in Port Blair, you will be transferred to Havelock Island. Upon arrival at the hotel in Havelock Island, check in and relax for a while. Later in the day, visit the famous Elephant Beach where you can indulge in adventurous water activities like snorkeling, scuba diving, undersea walking, and more.Afterwards, head to any of the restaurants or cafes to try the amazing seafood. In the evening,you will see the beautiful Radhnagar Beach where you get to spend some exclusive time as you soak in the breathtaking views of the sun going down the horizon. As the eventful day ends, you will be transferred to your hotel where you will get to dinner by a cozy overnight stay.


  • Day4-  Kickstart day 4 of the 6 nights 7 days by watching the mesmerizing sunrise view at the Kalapathar Beach in Havelock. Come back to the hotel for breakfast and then pack up and check out of the hotel.Next, this Andaman package moves to Neil Island. Check-in and get ready to explore the shimmering beaches of Neil Island - Sitapur Beach, Lakshmanpur Beach, and Bharatpur Beach. Conclude the day with a comfortable overnight stay at the hotel.


  • Day5-  Take a full day trip to the Baratang Islands off the coast of Port Blair After a healthy breakfast in Neil, you will be transferred back to Port Blair. From there, proceed for a full day tour of Baratang Island. You will get to visit some key attractions at Baratang including the limestone caves and mud volcanoes.As the day progresses, continue with the sightseeing tour and make the most of your holiday in Andaman. At the end of the day, return to the hotel for overnight stay.


  • Day6-  Take a day to relax and traverse the streets of Port Blair on your own Begin the penultimate day of your tour with an awesome breakfast. Then get ready to explore Port Blair on your own. You can take a city tour, go shopping and pick up some shell crafts and decoratives, or just chill out in a cafe or bar.Return to the hotel for an overnight stay.


  • Dav 7-  Your 6 nights 7 Days Andaman package comes to an end After breakfast at the hotel in Port Blair, you will prepare for checkout from the hotel. Post checkout, you will be transferred to the airport for your flight back.
