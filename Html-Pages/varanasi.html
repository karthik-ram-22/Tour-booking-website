Varanasi Tour Package (6 days 5 nights)


We offer a 6 day 5 night tour package to Varanasi and Buddha Gaya for devotees from Chennai and Mumbai via Airlines.


Iternary:


Varanasi(arrival)-Prayagraj-Ayodhya-Lucknow-Naimasharanya-Lucknow(Departure)


Tour plan:


  1. Day 1: Arriving at Varanasi


    Meet and greet at airport and transfer to pre booked Hotel . Assistance at check in at Hotel .Visiting Kashi Vishwanath temple after freshening up Visit to Annapurna devi temple , Vishalakshi temple and in the evening. take a boat ride in aarti ceremony at Dasaswmedh ghat . Overnight at Hotel in Varanasi.

  2. Day 2: Varanasi


    Pick up from Hotel in the morning and arrive for the sunrise boat tour on the Ganges where you could see the morning rituals .If you wish to take a holy dip in to the Ganges you can .Our boat ride covers most of the important ghat in Varanasi .post boat ride have a breakfast at Hotel and city tour of Varanasi which include Durga temple , Tulsi Manas temple , Sankat mochan temple , Banaras Hindu University and its temple , a silk weaving center where you could learn how sari are woven and have a lunch at local restaurant (at your own) and later on visit to Sarnath , a buddhist place where Lord Buddha gave his first sermon and visit include Buddha stupa , museum , deer park etc .Light and sound show in the evening . Overnight stay at Hotel in Varanasi.

  3. Day 3: Varanasi,Prayagraj,Ayodhya


    Today you will say good bye to the holy city of Varanasi after breakfast and your journey will continue to another important city also known as Tirth Raj the king of pligrimage Prayagraj where three holy rivers meet together and form the confluence known as sangam. upon arrival at Sangam take boat (direct payment ) for Sangam nose where the actual meeting pont of the rivers are.you can take a holy dip and perform the rituals and later on city sight of Prayagarj which are Hanuman temple , Akshay vat temple , Fort , Allepo devi temple and Swaraj Bhavan etc and later on drive to Ayodhya . you will be arriving Ayodhya a late night .upon arrival check in to Hotel and Night stay in Ayodhya.

  4. Day 4:Ayodhya-Lucknow


    In the morning after breakfast, check out from hotel and continue to city tour of Ayodhya which include Ram janm bhoomi , Hanuman Garhi , Sita Rasoi , Kanak Bhavan , Sugriv Bhavan , Dasrah mahal etc and drive to Lucknow. Upon arrival at Lucknow, check in to hotel and Night stay.

  5. Day 5:Lucknow-Naimasharanya


    In the morning post breakfast and proceed to sight seeing of Naimisharanaya including Trth Chakra, Hanuman Gaddi , Vyas Gaddi , Lalita Devi temple etc and later on drive to Lucknow. Overnight stay at Lucknow.

  6. Day 6:Lucknow(departure)


    After breakfast, half day sightseeing to lucknow . Later drop at airport for onward journey and here is the end of our services !!.


For traveling to the places of interest at the destination , you will be provided with a SUV.
